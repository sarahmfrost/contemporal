About the Project

Welcome to Contemporal.co - an interactive web application that showcases historic and current American Indian art and artists. We are interested in exploring the transmission of cultural material on the internet. As more and more people spend time on the internet, developments in technology allow novel art interactive experiences. This website can serve as an alternate market for many artists who have been impacted by COVID-19. We hope that this website will be educational and enjoyable. This web application was built during the summer of 2020 at the University of California, Santa Cruz.

If you are an American Indian artist and would like to showcase your art on this website, please contact sarahfrost@ucsc.edu.


Our website uses content from the National Museum of the American Indian website. Please refer to https://www.si.edu/termsofuse for more information about terms of use.
